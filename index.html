FIRST BLOG ENTRY

Well, HELLO! Lately i have been breaking out of a writing block and new ideas are coming to me daily. SO HAPPY to be writing music currently and sharing it with the world. I am so excited to start playing shows once i have enough for a set. So i will be releasing new songs until i have about 6-7 so i can rock out for 30 mins to an Hour at various places in akron, canton, beachwood, cleveland, stow, kent, OHIO. It has been some time since i have played live and am looking forward to getting back to it. I am so dedicated to this project and want to be a musician for life. It is my dream but i also know that even if your dream does not come true it is important to take joy in creating yourself and your music as you journey here through life. It is not about recognition, it is about sharing your soul with others and most importantly being happy with your creation. I get so much JOY writing these songs and listening to them. It makes me happy listening to my own song as an artist takes joy by looking at his completed peice of art whether it's a painting, song, a poem, performance, visual art etc. So i am doing this because it simply brings me joy, and if i can bring someone else joy through my music then, hell yeah! I want to say thank you for visiting my site and please if you dig the tunes tell someone about it, share it in a post, spread the word. Art is meant to be shared with the world not hidden in the dark. So anyways thanks for stopping by. YOU can listen to my music before it hits platforms like spotify googleplay etc. on
